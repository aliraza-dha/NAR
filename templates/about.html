Our Story

Founded in 2015, Najma al Raqeeb for Industry (NAR) emerged from a vision to revolutionize industrial manufacturing in the GCC region. What started as a small precision fabrication workshop has grown into a leading industrial manufacturing establishment.

Our journey began with a simple commitment: to deliver uncompromising quality through advanced technology and skilled craftsmanship. Today, we serve major industrial projects across Saudi Arabia and the broader GCC region.

With state-of-the-art Japanese and German machinery, ISO 9001:2015 certification, and a team of dedicated professionals, we continue to set new standards in precision metal fabrication and industrial solutions.
