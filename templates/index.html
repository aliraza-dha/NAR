About NAR Industry

Founded in 2015, Najma al Raqeeb for Industry (NAR) has established itself as a premier manufacturing establishment dedicated to industrial innovation and excellence in the GCC region.

Our enthusiastic team combines traditional craftsmanship with cutting-edge technology, delivering superior quality metal fabrication solutions that exceed client expectations. We specialize in precision engineering, custom fabrication, and industrial manufacturing services.

Key Achievements

  • ISO 9001:2015 Certified Quality Management
  • Advanced Japanese & German Machinery
  • 100% Quality Assurance Guaranteed
  • Serving Major GCC Industrial Projects
  • 24/7 Technical Support & Maintenance

Our Vision & Mission

To be the leading industrial manufacturing company in the GCC, recognized for innovation, quality, and customer satisfaction. We aim to revolutionize metal fabrication through continuous technological advancement and skilled craftsmanship.

Our mission is to provide precision-engineered solutions that empower industries, enhance operational efficiency, and contribute to regional industrial development.
